# Forgotten Rock Bands - Rock Music History

This project is a dedicated digital space for rediscovering and celebrating influential rock bands that have faded from the mainstream spotlight. From the experimental grooves of Krautrock to the complex compositions of Georgian progressive rock, this site serves as both a historical overview and a personal tribute to the "hidden gems" of the genre.


## 🎸 Featured Bands
The site provides detailed histories, discographies, and audio samples for several "great but forgotten" bands:
* **Birth Control (1966):** A German progressive and hard rock band known for heavy sound, complex compositions, and satirical lyrics[cite: 1, 2].
* **Can (1968):** Influential pioneers of the Krautrock movement who redefined rock by blending avant-garde experimentation, jazz-inspired improvisation, and electronic textures[cite: 1, 2].
* **Embryo (1969):** A musical collective that fused jazz, rock, and world music in ways that still feel ahead of their time[cite: 1, 2].
* **Focus (1969):** A Dutch band that stood apart by blending classical music, jazz improvisation, and rock virtuosity[cite: 1, 2].
* **Thunder Statue (1992):** A pioneering Georgian band that captured the sense of uncertainty and transformation during the crumbling of the Soviet Union[cite: 1, 2].

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **HTML5:** Structured with semantic elements like `<article>`, `<section>`, and `<aside>` for better organization[cite: 1].
* **CSS3:** Custom styling for a vintage rock aesthetic, featuring background textures and specialized layouts for audio-visual content[cite: 5].
* **Bootstrap 5.3.3:** Utilized for a responsive grid system, carousels, and interactive modals[cite: 1, 5].
* **Google Fonts:** Integrated "Yanone Kaffeesatz" and "Dekko" to enhance the visual character of the site[cite: 5].

## ♿ Accessibility Features
This project was built with web accessibility as a priority:
* **Skip Links:** Includes a "Skip to main content" link to assist keyboard-only users in navigating the site[cite: 1, 2, 3].
* **Video Descriptions:** A dedicated page provides a comprehensive textual description of the Rock History video, including visual and audio elements[cite: 4].
* **Semantic Markup:** Logical use of headings and landmarks ensures the site is navigable for screen reader users[cite: 1].

## 📂 Project Structure
* `index.html`: The homepage featuring a brief overview of rock history and an educational video[cite: 3].
* `forgotten-bands.html`: The main content hub featuring band profiles, audio players, and image galleries[cite: 1].
* `form.html`: A feedback platform for users to submit their own favorite forgotten bands[cite: 2].
* `video-description.html`: An accessibility-focused page providing a detailed timeline and description of the site's video content[cite: 4].
* `css/`: Directory containing modular stylesheets (`base.css`, `page-bands.css`, etc.)[cite: 1, 5].
